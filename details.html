Diet Plan Details

Click on a health issue below to view its diet plan

Diabetes

Eat whole grains, vegetables, lean proteins, and avoid refined sugar. Include bitter gourd, fenugreek seeds, and oats.

Heart Disease

Consume omega-3-rich foods (fish, flaxseeds), vegetables, and limit saturated fats & fried foods.

Obesity

Focus on calorie deficit, increase fruits & vegetables, drink plenty of water, and avoid sugary drinks.

Anemia

Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, beetroot, lentils, and lean meats. Add vitamin C foods to improve absorption.

High Blood Pressure

Follow the DASH diet: more fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and reduce salt. Avoid processed foods.

PCOS

Include whole grains, lean proteins, and fiber-rich foods. Limit refined carbs and sugary snacks.

Thyroid Issues

Eat iodine-rich foods like seaweed, dairy, and eggs. Selenium-rich foods like Brazil nuts support thyroid function.

Digestive Problems

Eat probiotic foods like yogurt, kefir, and fermented vegetables. Avoid spicy and oily foods.

Kidney Disease

Limit sodium, potassium, and phosphorus. Eat cauliflower, berries, garlic, and egg whites.

Asthma

Include vitamin C-rich fruits, leafy greens, and omega-3 foods. Avoid processed foods and sulfites.

Osteoporosis

Eat calcium-rich foods like dairy, almonds, tofu, and green leafy vegetables. Ensure vitamin D intake.

Cancer Prevention

Eat antioxidant-rich foods like berries, broccoli, garlic, and tomatoes. Avoid processed meats & alcohol.

Depression & Anxiety

Consume omega-3s (fish, walnuts), whole grains, leafy greens, and dark chocolate. Avoid caffeine overload.

Arthritis

Eat anti-inflammatory foods like fatty fish, turmeric, ginger, and olive oil. Avoid processed foods and red meats.

Liver Disease

Eat leafy greens, garlic, avocado, nuts, and coffee (in moderation). Avoid alcohol and fried foods.
